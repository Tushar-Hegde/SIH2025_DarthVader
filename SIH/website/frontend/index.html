Common Pests & Treatment

Identify and manage agricultural pests with recommended pesticide solutions

Aphids

(Sitobion avenae)

Common Pest

Recommended Pesticides:

  • Dimethoate - Systemic insecticide
  • Imidacloprid - Neonicotinoid insecticide
Apply during early morning or evening for best results

Pink Bollworm

(Pectinophora gossypiella)

Major Cotton Pest

Recommended Pesticides:

  • Chlorantraniliprole - Selective insecticide
  • Cypermethrin - Pyrethroid insecticide
  • Emamectin benzoate - Biological insecticide
Monitor cotton bolls regularly during flowering stage

Early Shoot Borer

(Chilo infuscatellus)

Sugarcane Pest

Recommended Pesticides:

  • Chlorpyrifos - Organophosphate insecticide
  • Carbofuran - Systemic insecticide
Best applied at early crop stage (2-3 months)

Important Safety Notice

Always follow manufacturer guidelines for pesticide application. Use appropriate protective equipment and maintain recommended waiting periods before harvest.
